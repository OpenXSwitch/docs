<Tip>
  📘 **Built-in Gas Infrastructure**\
  **Wallet** **products** across **OpenXSwitch** include built-in gas management and transaction sponsorship across supported blockchain networks, abstracting native gas mechanics and simplifying transaction execution.
</Tip>

- **Wallet-as-a-Service** sandbox environments operate as simulated off-chain environments.
- **Smart Accounts** testnet environments use supported blockchain-native testnets.
- Blockchain availability may vary depending on workspace configuration and infrastructure enablement.
- Token support varies by blockchain network and product.
