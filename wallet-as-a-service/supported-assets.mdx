In addition to native assets, Wallet-as-a-Service Infrastructure supports a set of rechargeable tokens for:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Internal transfers
- Instant swaps
- Trading operations

<Tip>
  Tokens listed here are recharge-enabled assets with support for both deposits and withdrawals. Additional assets may also be supported exclusively for trading or instant swap operations.
</Tip>

> Use the [Get Recharge Currencies](https://docs.openxswitch.com/api-reference/mix-endpoints/get-list-of-rechargeable-currencies) endpoint to retrieve the latest supported assets, chains, limits, confirmations, and withdrawal fee metadata.
